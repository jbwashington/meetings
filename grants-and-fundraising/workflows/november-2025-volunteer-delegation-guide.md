## üí° General Tips for All Volunteers

### Time Management:
- **Block focused time:** Turn off phone, close email, eliminate distractions
- **Best times:** Early morning (7-9am) or evening (8-10pm) for quiet work
- **Break tasks into chunks:** 30-45 min sessions with breaks
- **Use weekends** for tasks requiring phone calls or external coordination

### Communication Best Practices:
- **Be specific in requests:** "Need X by Y date" not "Need X soon"
- **Follow up appropriately:** 3 days for emails, 2 days for urgent
- **Thank people promptly:** Within 24 hours of receiving help
- **CC James** on important emails so he's in the loop

### When You're Stuck:
1. **Reread instructions** - answer might be there
2. **Google it** - many questions have online answers
3. **Ask James** - he's here to support you
4. **Don't wait too long** - speak up early if confused
5. **It's okay to say "I need help"** - better than missing deadline

### Quality Standards:
- **Good enough is good enough** - perfectionism slows progress
- **Proofread once** - catch obvious errors but don't obsess
- **Check your math** - especially for budgets and quantities
- **Verify dates** - easy to mix up deadlines
- **Save often** - don't lose work to technical issues

### Self-Care:
- **Don't overcommit** - be realistic about your time
- **Take breaks** - step away when frustrated
- **Ask for help** - delegate if you're overwhelmed
- **Celebrate small wins** - every completed task matters
- **Remember the impact** - your work helps 350+ students
